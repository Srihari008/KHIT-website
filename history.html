Our History

Kallam Haranadhreddy Institute of Technology (KHIT) was established with a vision to provide quality technical education and create skilled professionals who contribute to the nation's development. Founded by the visionary industrialist and philanthropist, Sri Kallam Haranadhreddy, the institution has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the premier engineering colleges in Andhra Pradesh.

The college was established under the aegis of the Kallam Haranadhreddy Educational Society, which has been committed to educational excellence and social development for over two decades. Our journey reflects the dedication, perseverance, and commitment to academic excellence that has been the cornerstone of our institution.

1998
1998
Foundation Laid
The foundation stone for Kallam Haranadhreddy Institute of Technology was laid by Sri Kallam Haranadhreddy, marking the beginning of a new era in technical education in the Guntur region.
2001
2001
Institution Established
KHIT was officially established and started its first academic year with three core engineering branches: Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical Engineering.
2005
2005
AICTE Recognition
The institution received full recognition from the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and was affiliated with JNTU Kakinada, marking a significant milestone in our academic journey.
2008
2008
Infrastructure Expansion
Major infrastructure development including state-of-the-art laboratories, modern classrooms, and a central library with extensive collection of books and digital resources.
2012
2012
New Departments Added
Introduction of Civil Engineering and Electronics & Communication Engineering departments, expanding our academic offerings and providing more opportunities for students.
2015
2015
NBA Accreditation
The Computer Science and Mechanical Engineering departments received NBA (National Board of Accreditation) accreditation, recognizing the quality of our technical education.
2018
2018
Research & Development
Establishment of Research & Development center and introduction of M.Tech programs, fostering innovation and advanced studies in engineering disciplines.
2021
2021
Digital Transformation
Complete digital transformation with smart classrooms, online learning platforms, and advanced IT infrastructure to meet the challenges of modern education.
2024

Excellence & Growth
Celebrating over two decades of excellence in technical education, with thousands of successful alumni working in leading companies worldwide and contributing to society.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of higher learning, committed to academic excellence, innovation, and research, producing competent professionals who contribute to the sustainable development of society and the nation.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education through innovative teaching-learning processes, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and industry collaboration, fostering creativity, leadership, and ethical values among students to meet global challenges.
